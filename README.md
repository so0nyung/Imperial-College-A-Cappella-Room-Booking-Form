# Imperial-College-A-Cappella-Room-Booking-Form
### Foreword
Yes, Imperial College London is not beating the stereotypes.

Enjoy me opening a lot of issues with Imperial College Union by making this repository. Although in fairness there's a chance that next year they'll just abandon this website and the whole cycle of trying to book rooms for 6 groups who rehearse twice a week for 38 weeks starts again. (That's 456 room booking attempts at a minimum. This excludes all the back and forth with finding a new room if something goes wrong)

Anyways if you couldn't tell, I made the repo so that, if union sticks to the current system, future A Cappella secretarys can use it to ease the pain of room bookings. No guarantees though.

## STATEMENT
This repository is public facing and can be accessed to the public. However, I have no malicious intentions, nor have any intentions to encourage people to exploit such vulnerabilities. I am simply a STEM student, who is trying to help a society deal with an issue that our Union can't/ won't help with. I can't afford to maintain a website with my money, especially knowing that the form might not even be up for more than a few years.
